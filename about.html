Little Story

Yamifood Restaurant was born in 2000. We are an unavoidable place for all adventurers, looking for a good bite and positive energy.

Contact us

The ambiance of Yamifood Restaurant, in which our guests feel comfortable at home, has been created with dedication. We have tried to satisfy all your needs. We breathed in his measured intimacy and enriched it with a touch of our history. In such an ambience, our culinary knight, Mr. Slobodan Miljaković, reigns, who with his young team creates real small masterpieces of national cuisine. With great enthusiasm, in which they have woven their skills, they prepare our traditional dishes and thus keep them a bit from oblivion. You will enjoy home cooking here at ours.

Yamifood Restaurant uses local farmer's products whenever possible to provide fresh, sustainable dishes. Our chef meticulously pairs the ingredients to offer the best dining experience possible. Yamifood prides itself on keeping its ingredients and collaborations local. From the gracious service to the charming atmosphere, Yamifood certainly strives to provide a spectacular overall dining experience.

Staff

These are just some of our experts.

There are several categories of personnel in the restaurant business: managers, cooks, servers, buspersons, dishwashers, hosts and bartenders. Each has a specific function and contributes to the operation of the restaurant.

Our team of experts in cuisine, consisting of William Nelson, Christian Justice, Steve Thomson and many others, who show their knowledge and skills every day at our restaurant and who have acquired their education at prestigious culinary universities, will give you an unforgettable experience in the form of delicious food and a variety of specialties.
